If you hold an index CFD position overnight when a dividend adjustment is applied, the following may occur:

- Buy positions may receive a dividend adjustment
- Sell positions may incur a dividend-related charge

These adjustments reflect the impact of dividends paid by the underlying index constituents and are applied automatically to open positions.
