## A Quick Introduction to Global Markets

The trading world offers several major markets, each with its own behavior, risks, and opportunities.  
Here’s a simple and concise overview of the four markets most new traders explore.

---

## **1. Forex: Currency Trading**
Forex is the global market for exchanging one currency for another.  
It operates **24 hours a day, 5 days a week** and is the most liquid market in the world.

### Why Traders Like Forex
- High liquidity  
- Low spreads on major pairs  
- Flexible trading hours  
- Opportunity to trade rising and falling markets  

Common pairs include **EUR/USD**, **GBP/USD**, and **USD/JPY**.

---

## **2. Metals: Gold & Silver**
Precious metals like **gold (XAU)** and **silver (XAG)** are popular safe-haven assets.  
They tend to hold value during uncertainty and provide diversification.

### Key Drivers
- Inflation expectations  
- Interest rates  
- Geopolitical events  
- Risk-on / risk-off sentiment  

Gold is often seen as a hedge against economic instability.

---

## **3. Crypto: Digital Assets**
Cryptocurrencies such as **Bitcoin** and **Ethereum** trade **24/7** and are known for their high volatility.  
This creates both opportunity and risk.

### What Moves Crypto?
- Market sentiment  
- Technology updates  
- Regulation news  
- Institutional adoption  

Crypto can experience large price swings in short time periods—risk management is essential.

---

## **4. Commodities: Energy & Agriculture**
Commodities include energy products (oil, gas) and agricultural goods (wheat, corn).  
Their prices reflect real-world supply and demand.

### What Affects Commodity Prices?
- Weather and seasons  
- Production levels  
- Geopolitical tensions  
- Global economic health  

Crude oil (WTI/Brent) and natural gas are among the most traded.

---

## Final Thoughts

Forex, metals, crypto, and commodities each offer unique opportunities.  
The best market for you depends on your **risk tolerance**, **time availability**, and **interest**.

Start simple, learn the basics, and always trade with a plan.
