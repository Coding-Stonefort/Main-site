Understanding Forex Leverage and Margin

Leverage and margin are powerful tools in Forex trading, but they also come with risks. Here's a breakdown:

Leverage: Leverage allows traders to control a larger position with a smaller amount of capital. For example, with 100:1 leverage, you can control $100,000 with just $1,000 in your account.

Margin: Margin is the amount of capital you need to open and maintain a leveraged position. It’s essentially a good faith deposit to open a trade.

While leverage can amplify profits, it can also magnify losses. Always use leverage cautiously and ensure you're aware of the risks before jumping in.
