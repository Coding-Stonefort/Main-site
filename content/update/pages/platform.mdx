# Platforms FAQs – Your Questions Answered

## How do I log in to my Stonefort trading account?

You can log in via the **Stonefort Client Portal** or directly through your chosen platform, including MetaTrader 5, the Web Trading Platform, or the Stonefort Trader mobile app. Step-by-step guidance is available in the client portal.

---

## What are Expert Advisors?

Expert Advisors (EAs) are automated trading programs that execute strategies based on predefined rules. On MetaTrader 5, EAs can manage trade execution, risk, and strategy testing.

EAs run locally on your device and require an active server connection. Multiple EAs can be used simultaneously when applied to separate charts.
