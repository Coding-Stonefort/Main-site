# Forex Basics for Beginners

Forex (FX) is the global marketplace for buying and selling currencies...

## Why Forex Is Popular

- High liquidity  
- 24/5 markets  
- Low entry barriers  
- Leverage  

## Key Terms

### **Pip**
A pip is the smallest change in a currency pair’s price.

### **Lot**
A lot is the standardized trading size in Forex.

### **Spread**
The difference between *bid* and *ask*.
