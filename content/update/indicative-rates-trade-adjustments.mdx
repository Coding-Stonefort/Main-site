All rates shown are indicative and may change based on market conditions. Final pricing is applied at the server rollover time. Any profits or losses from index CFD trades will be reflected in your account balance with the relevant trade comments.

Traders are encouraged to regularly monitor open positions and stay informed about market updates that may affect their trades.
