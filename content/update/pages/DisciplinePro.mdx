# DisciplinePro
## Trade with Discipline. Perform with Confidence.

In forex trading, no two sessions are ever the same. Volatility, breaking news, and emotions can challenge even experienced traders. **DisciplinePro** is designed to keep your decision-making structured, focused, and aligned with your trading objectives — regardless of market conditions.

By delivering **real-time insights into your trading behaviour**, DisciplinePro helps you identify discipline gaps, manage risk with clarity, and avoid impulsive decisions that often lead to unnecessary losses. It acts as a silent trading companion, reinforcing consistency while you remain fully in control of execution.

## How DisciplinePro Works

DisciplinePro monitors every trade against your **defined trading plan**. Each closed position is evaluated based on **discipline and rule adherence**, regardless of whether the trade ends in profit or loss.

This approach helps shift your focus from short-term outcomes to **decision quality**, reinforcing long-term consistency and professional trading habits.

You can access DisciplinePro through **Performance Analytics**.

## How often should I use DisciplinePro?
DisciplinePro is designed to support your trading routine at every stage:

### At the start of each trading day
Review your daily risk parameters, calculated based on your account balance and closed positions as of midnight. This helps ensure your position sizing remains aligned with available capital.

### During live trading
Keep the Discipline Monitor active to receive alerts if your trading behaviour approaches a breach of defined rules.

### At the end of the day, week, or month
Review your **Discipline Score** to evaluate consistency, identify behavioural patterns, and refine your trading approach over time.

Regular use of DisciplinePro helps protect capital and reinforces disciplined execution in changing market conditions.
