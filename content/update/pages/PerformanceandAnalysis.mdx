# Performance Analytics – Frequently Asked Questions

## What are the requirements to access Performance Analytics?
You can access Performance Analytics by logging into **Web Trader**, opening your workspace, clicking the three-dot menu, and selecting **Performance Analytics**.

On the mobile app:  
**More → Performance Analytics → My Trading History**

---

## How much does Performance Analytics cost?
Performance Analytics is available **at no additional cost** to eligible live account holders.

---

## Can I use Performance Analytics with a demo account?
No. Performance Analytics is **not currently available** on demo accounts.

---

## Can I access Performance Analytics via the mobile app?
Yes. On the mobile app, select **More**, then go to **Performance Analytics** under **My Trading History**.

---

## Are there tutorials available on how to use Performance Analytics?
Yes. Platform video tutorials are available and explain features and functionality step by step.

---

## Is the information real-time or delayed?
When first accessing Performance Analytics, there may be a short delay while historical trade data is processed.  
After that, updates are **near real-time**, typically delayed by only a few minutes.

---

## Why am I not seeing any insights or trading edges yet?
Performance Analytics requires a **minimum amount of closed trade data** to generate meaningful insights.  
If you haven’t closed enough trades, some analytics may not yet appear.

---

## If I have multiple accounts, can I view them together?
No. Performance Analytics is **account-specific**. You must log in to each account individually.

---

## Can I export performance data from Performance Analytics?
Yes. You can export data in **CSV format**.

Go to **Statements**, select a date range, and click **Export**.

---

## What does it mean if no positive or negative habits are shown under Psychology Edges?
This indicates there is **not yet enough completed trade data** to identify behavioural patterns.

---

## Why does the P&L differ from my account trade history?
Performance Analytics reviews up to **12 months of trade data**, capped at **10,000 transactions per account**.

P&L calculations include **trade openings and closings only** and exclude:
- Financing costs  
- Commissions  
- Rebates  
- Dividend adjustments
