Day trading is an active trading approach where all positions are opened and closed within the same trading day. This allows traders to take advantage of short-term price movements in global indices while avoiding overnight market risk.

Successful index day trading relies on fast execution, reliable pricing, advanced charting tools, and low trading costs. While indices offer strong intraday opportunities, many traders underestimate the discipline required. Consistent results depend on proper education, a clear trading plan, effective risk management, and emotional control.
