# Web Trader – Frequently Asked Questions

## How current is the information on Web Trader?
Web Trader uses **real-time pricing data**, allowing you to trade on live market prices across all available instruments. Live pricing is also available on demo accounts to closely reflect real trading conditions.

---

## What browsers are supported?
Web Trader is compatible with all modern browsers, including **Google Chrome**, **Microsoft Edge**, and **Mozilla Firefox**. For optimal performance and security, we recommend using the latest browser version.

---

## What can I trade on Web Trader?
You can trade a wide range of instruments, including **80+ FX pairs**, as well as **gold and silver**.

To explore all available markets, log in and select the **Range of Markets** tab. You can browse by asset class or view trending instruments from the side menu.

---

## Is Web Trader secure?
Yes. Secure data transmission protocols are used across all platforms to ensure a safe and protected trading environment.

---

## What features does Web Trader offer?
Web Trader includes a comprehensive set of professional tools, such as:
- Advanced charting tools  
- Drag-and-drop order editing  
- 50+ technical indicators  
- Market comparison functionality  
- Customisable and saved layouts  
- Integrated market analysis and economic calendar  
- Full account management access  

---

## Can I manage my funds on Web Trader?
Yes. You can deposit funds or submit withdrawal requests directly via **MyAccount**, accessible within the Web Trader platform.

---

## How do I add a market to my Watchlist?
Type the market name into the search field at the top of a watchlist and select it.  
Alternatively, from the browse or search menu, click the **“+”** icon and choose the watchlist where you want the market added.

---

## How do I change my layout?
Web Trader layouts are fully customisable. You can resize and reposition windows, create multiple layouts within a workspace, and switch easily between different workspaces.

---

## How do I compare multiple markets on the same chart?
Select the **Compare** option at the top of the chart, then enter the market symbol you wish to compare. Multiple markets can be overlaid on the same chart.

---

## How do I add a Stop or Limit order to an open position?
Go to the **Positions** tab and click within the stop or limit field of the relevant position.

*Please note: contingent orders help manage risk but do not guarantee loss limitation under all market conditions.*

---

## How do I log out of Web Trader?
Click on the **Account** icon and select **Log Out**.
